South Asian Heart Risk Check

Standard cholesterol tests and risk calculators often miss the patterns that drive heart disease in South Asian populations. This tool helps identify what to look for — and what to discuss with your physician.

Educational only — not a diagnostic tool, not a substitute for medical advice. Most relevant for South Asian adults 35 and older, or younger adults with a family history of premature heart disease.
Murtaza Bharmal, MD · Interventional Cardiologist · Last reviewed March 2026

About

Where Engineering Precision Meets Clinical Excellence

Dr. Murtaza Bharmal is a board-certified Interventional Cardiologist practicing in Fullerton, California, affiliated with Providence St. Jude Medical Center and UC Irvine Medical Center. His career is defined by a rare combination of engineering rigor, academic excellence, and unwavering patient-centered care.

Before medicine, Dr. Bharmal earned a BS in Biomedical Engineering with honors from UC San Diego and worked as an R&D engineer at Abbott Vascular ‚Äî giving him a distinctive foundation in the technology that drives modern cardiology.

He earned his MD from the University of Vermont College of Medicine, where he was elected Class President of the prestigious Alpha Omega Alpha (AOA) Honor Society. He completed Internal Medicine residency at the University of Chicago and his Fellowship at UC Irvine, where he served as Chief Fellow.
